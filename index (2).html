what is Anime?

According to the Japanese concept of anime, this term is used to classify all types of animation, that is, regardless of their origin (national or foreign). The origin of the word anime is uncertain. Some researchers consider it to be an abbreviation of the English animation (“animation”), while others argue that it may have emerged from the French animée (“animated”). Anime can be of numerous different types, such as children's, horror, romance, comedy, adventure, erotic, science fiction, etc.

Features of anime

Anime drawings are characterized by the intense demonstration of the characters' feelings and emotions through their facial expressions. Big eyes, full of brightness and color, onomatopoeia and exaggerated expressions are some of the most common characteristics in anime.

Top 3 Studios

Mappa

MAPPA Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded on June 14, 2011 by Masao Maruyama, founder and former producer of Madhouse. "MAPPA" is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.

Ufotable

Ufotable is a Japanese animation studio based in Suginami, Tokyo. Hikaru Kondou - founded the studio in October 2000. The company's name originates from a UFO-shaped table that Kondou found while developing the studio's vision.

Pierrot

Pierrot Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded in May 1979 by former employees of Tatsunoko Production and Mushi Production. Pierrot is known for several series of popular anime around the world.

Animes

jujutsu kaisen

Sinopse

"Jujutsu Kaisen" follows Yuji Itadori, drawn into a world of curses after encountering a malevolent object. Enrolling in Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School, he, alongside classmates Megumi and the eccentric Satoru Gojo, battles Cursed Spirits. As they confront escalating threats, the trio grapples with the consequences of their actions, exploring themes of friendship and the fine line between humanity and malevolence. With dynamic action and well-developed characters, "Jujutsu Kaisen" has become a standout in anime, combining intense battles with a compelling narrative in its exploration of the supernatural..

Naruto

Sinopse

Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage, faces various challenges and adversaries in his journey. Orphaned at a young age and having the powerful Nine-Tailed Fox sealed within him, Naruto strives to gain recognition from his fellow villagers and prove his worth. Along with his friends Sasuke and Sakura, Naruto embarks on a path of self-discovery, growth, and the pursuit of his ninja dreams.

One Piece

Sinopse

Monkey D. Luffy, a young and ambitious pirate, sets out on a journey to find the legendary treasure known as One Piece and become the Pirate King. Along the way, he forms a diverse and powerful crew, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, and they navigate through the Grand Line, facing powerful enemies, making new friends, and discovering the mysteries of the world. With a dream as vast as the ocean, Luffy and his crew embark on an epic adventure that will test their strength, determination, and friendship.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

Sinopse

"I Want to Eat Your Pancreas" is a poignant and emotional anime that revolves around the unexpected bond between an introverted high school student known only as "Me" and Sakura Yamauchi, a girl suffering from a pancreatic illness. After discovering Sakura's secret diary, Me learns about her limited time left and decides to spend her remaining days fulfilling her wishes. The story explores themes of friendship, love, and the impact of terminal illness on the lives of those affected. Prepare for a heartfelt journey as Me and Sakura embark on an unforgettable and bittersweet adventure.

Nana

Sinopse

"Nana" follows the intertwined lives of two young women with the same name who meet by chance on a train to Tokyo. Nana Osaki, a punk rock vocalist with dreams of stardom, and Nana Komatsu, a more innocent and optimistic girl, form an unlikely friendship as they navigate the challenges of love, friendship, and pursuing their aspirations in the bustling city. The series delves into themes of relationships, personal growth, and the complexities of the entertainment industry. With a soundtrack featuring rock music and a compelling narrative, "Nana" offers a captivating and emotional journey through the lives of its two protagonists.

Love, Chunibyo & Other Delusions

Synopsis

"Love, Chunibyo & Other Delusions" follows the story of Yuuta Togashi, a high school student trying to leave behind his embarrassing past as a "chunibyo" — someone who harbors delusions of grandeur and fantasy. In an attempt to lead a normal high school life, Yuuta encounters Rikka Takanashi, a girl still fully immersed in her delusional world.

Death Note

Sinopse

Light Yagami, a highly intelligent and bored high school student, discovers a mysterious notebook known as the Death Note. The Death Note has the power to kill anyone whose name is written in it, as long as the writer has the person's face in mind. Light, who aspires to create a utopia by eliminating criminals, begins using the Death Note to mete out his own form of justice. However, his actions attract the attention of the enigmatic and highly skilled detective known only as "L." What follows is a high-stakes game of cat and mouse between Light and L, with the fate of the world hanging in the balance.

One Punch Man

Sinopse

Saitama, a seemingly ordinary man, becomes a hero for fun and sets out to find a worthy opponent. However, he becomes so powerful that he can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Frustrated by the lack of challenging battles, Saitama faces an existential crisis despite his overwhelming strength. Alongside his cyborg disciple Genos, Saitama joins the Hero Association to gain recognition for his heroic deeds and seeks to uncover the secrets behind his unprecedented strength. "One Punch Man" is a satirical take on the superhero genre, blending action, humor, and a unique perspective on the concept of power in the world of heroes and villains.

My Hero Academia

Sinopse

In a world where nearly everyone possesses superpowers known as "Quirks," Izuku Midoriya is born without any special abilities. Despite facing numerous challenges and discrimination for being Quirkless, Izuku remains determined to enroll in U.A. High School, the prestigious academy for aspiring heroes. His life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters the legendary hero All Might, who chooses him as his successor to inherit the powerful Quirk "One For All." Now, Izuku embarks on a journey to become a hero and faces formidable villains along with his classmates, each with their unique Quirks. "My Hero Academia" explores themes of heroism, friendship, and the pursuit of one's dreams in a world full of superpowers.
